Typography

For the logo, we chose the "Zion" font as it captures adventure, ideal for Questify's playful and educational exploration theme.

For the content of the app, we are using "Sora". The font offers readability and use-friendliness as it is sans-serif, enhancing user experience and app's inviting atmosphere.

PaytoneOne's boldness enhances accessibility and usability, ensuring clarity and easy navigation for important headings and menu text in the app.

For the making of the icons we understand that inorder to create a good UX we need to ensure that icons/ positions and everything we design fits the user percieved usbility. We did a brief researc of what a few competitors tend to use for their icons and these are the icons we had decided to use for our app.

For home we decided to use a house icon. While for about about we use a question mark as it is a common symbol for questions. For FAQ we use an exclamation point, to indicate things users might want to know. For our quest we used two little cards to indicate the physical form of quizzes. For learning we use a very obvious book to indicate learning. While lastly a typical gear icon to represent settings to match user expectancy.

Mascot

This is Jayc, Quesetify's very own mascot!

Jayc embodies the spirit of adventure and learning. With his vibrant yellow helmet and blue flag featuring a tree emblem, Jayc symbolizes the journey of discovery that users embark upon when using our app.

The yellow helmet represents optimism, energy, and enthusiasm, encouraging users to engage with our educational content with excitement. Meanwhile, the blue flag with a tree emblem signifies our app's focus on nature and environmental awareness, reflecting Vancouver's natural landscapes and green spaces. Together, Jayc's colors create a visually appealing representation of our app's mission to educate and inspire users about Vancouver's cultural and natural heritage.
